:clan: IN-GAME CLAN RULES

1. Activity & Participation

The Inactivity Limit: Players inactive for 5 consecutive days without notifying leadership will be kicked.

Presence: Popping into the chat occasionally shows you're alive and engaged. Even if just in the Discord! We aren't too strict on this.

Upgrading: Keep your builders busy! Rushed bases (unless donation accounts) may be excluded from wars.

2. Donations & Requests

Read Before You Donate: Always donate exactly what is requested. No "surprise" Wall Breakers when Dragons are asked for.

Ratios: No strict ratios, but heavy leeching without giving back will result in a warning.

War Donations: Strictly MAX level troops only for War CCs and War attacks.

3. Clan Wars & CWL

Opting In/Out: Keep your shield updated. Red if Heroes/Spells are upgrading; Green if ready.

Attack Usage: Use both attacks regardless of the score. Missing attacks is a high offense.

Targeting: Follow the war notes or attack your mirror for the first hit. Clean-up for the second.

No Heroes, No War: Standard wars require Heroes to be up.

4. Clan Games & Raids

Clan Games: Minimum 1000 points required. We always aim for the top tier.

Capital Raids: Use all 5-6 attacks every weekend. Finish the district you start.

5. Promotions

Earned, Not Given: Elder is based on consistency, donations, and war performance. Co-Leader is for trusted long-term staff.

💬 DISCORD SERVER RULES

1. Verification

Restricted Access: Unverified users cannot use bots or see core channels. Verify by being in the clan and requesting a role.

2. Community Conduct

Zero Tolerance: No hate speech or toxicity. Banter is fine; harassment is an instant ban.

Keep it Clean: No NSFW content, intense politics, or religious debates.

3. Etiquette

Stay on Topic: Use specific channels (#base-designs, #bot-commands) for their intended purpose.

Bot Spam: Keep interactions in #bot-commands only.

4. Elders and Co-Owner Requirements

Discord Required: You will need to be in our server with a discord account. And must be well known to recieve any roles.

SIGN-UP Form: Currently no way to apply, We go off if we know you for now.

5. Pinging

@everyone: Leadership only.

Pinging Staff: Only for time-sensitive emergencies.

6. Discord Protection Rule (Locked-In!)

Protection: 🔥 We value communication! If you take the time to join this Discord, your account is officially "Locked In" and protected from sudden kicks.

How it works: If you aren't in the Discord, 5 days of in-game inactivity means an automatic kick. But if you are in here with us, we know real life happens. If you go inactive for 5 days, we will check on you by pinging you in the server. You May recieve warnings. But As long as you reply or log in, you're safe. We only kick Discord members if they completely ghost the warnings.

🛡️ LEADERSHIP & PROMOTIONS

Becoming an Elder

Loyalty: Must stay in the clan without "hopping" for at least 90 consecutive days.

Participation: Maximize points in Clan Games and use all attacks in Raid Weekends.

Donations: Maintain a healthy donation-to-request ratio.

Behavior: Follow all clan rules, be helpful to new members, and avoid drama in the chat.

Becoming a Co-Leader

Extreme Trust: Usually reserved for long-term members who have proven their reliability over several months.

Leadership Qualities: Must be able to manage wars, send helpful Clan Mails, and assist in recruiting.

War Consistency: Flawless attendance and high-star performance in Clan War Leagues (CWL) for at least two or more cycles.

Communication: We have a discord in order to be either an Elder or Co-Leader you must be joined into the discord.

⚙️ DEFAULT ROLE PERMISSIONS

Elder Permissions

Invite & Accept: Can invite players and accept/decline join requests.

Kick Members: Can kick regular Members, but not other Elders or Co-Leaders.

Promote: Can promote Members to Elder status.

Co-Leader Permissions

Full Management: Can invite, accept, or kick both Members and Elders.

War Control: Start Clan Wars, choose participants, and manage the war map.

Clan Mail: Send mass messages to all members (typically with a 1-hour cooldown).

Settings: Edit the Clan Notice board and change most clan settings (trophy requirements, clan type).

Promote/Demote: Can promote/demote Elders and promote new Co-Leaders.

📜 ELDER & CO-LEADER RULES

Elder Rules

No Power Tripping: Never kick a member for a personal grudge. Kicking must always be performance or rule-based like being toxic. Disrespectful, inactive etc.

No False Promises: Do not promise promotions to other members. Only Co-Leaders and the Leader can authorize promotions.

No Silent Kicks: If you kick someone, you must post the reason in the Discord #kick-logs channel so the Co-Leaders know why. This is required for Elders to prevent false banning.

Co-Leader Rules

No Ghosting: If you are going to be away for more than 3-5 days, you must post in the #leave-of-absence (LOA) channel.

No Rogue Changes: Do not change the Clan Settings (Trophy requirements, Clan Type, or Description) without a majority vote from the other Co-Leaders in the private Discord chat.

No Mass Kicking: Kicking more than 3 people in a single day requires a discussion with the Leader first to ensure the clan's stability.

⚖️ CONSEQUENCES & PUNISHMENTS

1. Minor Infractions

Infractions: Low donations, missing one war attack, mild attitude.

Elder: A formal warning in Discord. Second offense results in a 3-day demotion to Member. Third offense is a demotion for 1 week. And fourth offense is a demotion to member and loss of staff permanently.

Co-Leader: A private discussion with the Leader. Second offense results in demotion to Elder Until further decided.

2. Major Infractions

Infractions: Ignoring war plans, missing multiple attacks, "Ghosting".

Elder: Immediate demotion to Member and loss of Elder permissions for one full season.

Co-Leader: Immediate demotion to Elder. Must re-earn trust over a 1-month period to be considered for Co-Leader again.

3. Abuse of Power

Infractions: Kicking active members without cause, changing clan settings to "Troll," Toxicity.

Both Roles: Instant Permanent Ban from the Clan and the Discord Server. There is a zero-tolerance policy for sabotaging the clan's progress. And we take this very seriously.
